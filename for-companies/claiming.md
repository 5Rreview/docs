# Claiming Your Profile

How to take ownership of your company profile on 5RRRRR.

## Why Claim?

Claiming your profile gives you access to:
- **Respond to reviews** publicly
- **View your trust score** breakdown
- **Access analytics** (with paid features)
- **Customize your profile** with logo, description, and media
- **Track resolution** of customer issues

## How to Claim

1. **Find your company** — search for your company on 5RRRRR.com
2. **Click "Claim This Profile"** — on your company page
3. **Verify ownership** — prove you represent the company
4. **Set up your account** — configure your profile and response team

## Verification Methods

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Email verification | Verify using an email on the company domain |
| Domain verification | Add a DNS TXT record to your domain |
| Document verification | Upload official business documents |

## After Claiming

### Free Features (Always Available)
- Respond to all reviews
- View trust score and public metrics
- Basic profile customization
- Resolution tracking

### Paid Features (Optional)
- Analytics dashboard (sentiment trends, competitor benchmarks)
- Priority response tools (queue management, templates, team assignment)
- CRM integration (customer tracking, issue management)
- Advanced brand profile (media gallery, featured content)
- Data export and API access

**Important:** Paid features provide tools and insights. They never affect your trust score, ranking, or review visibility. See [Sacred Rule #1](https://github.com/5Rreview/open-trust-methodology/blob/main/docs/sacred-rules.md).

## Multiple Locations

If your company has multiple locations, each can be claimed and managed separately or as a group under a single account.
