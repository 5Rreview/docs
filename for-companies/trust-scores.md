# 🛡️ How 5R★ Trust Score Works

A guide for companies on what affects your trust score on [5rrrrr.com](https://5rrrrr.com).

## What is a 5R★ Trust Score?

Your trust score (0–100) represents how trustworthy your company appears based on all available evidence. It's calculated algorithmically — no human manually sets it, and no payment can influence it.

## What Affects Your Score

### ✅ Positive Signals

| Signal | Impact | What to do |
|--------|--------|------------|
| High review ratings | Major | Deliver great service consistently |
| High response rate | Significant | Respond to every review, especially negative ones |
| Issue resolution | Significant | Resolve problems and let reviewers update their ratings |
| Review volume | Moderate | Encourage satisfied customers to share their experience |
| Review quality | Minor | High-quality detailed reviews carry more weight |
| Verified reviews | Moderate | Verified purchases are more influential |

### 🚫 What Does NOT Affect Your Score

- Whether you use paid features (analytics, CRM, branding)
- Your company's size or industry
- How long you've been on the platform
- Advertising spend
- Any business relationship with 5R★

This is our [Sacred Rule #1](https://github.com/5Rreview/open-trust-methodology/blob/main/docs/sacred-rules.md).

## 📊 Confidence Levels

Your score includes a confidence indicator based on review volume:

| Volume | Confidence | Display |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Very few reviews | Low | "Not Yet Rated" — score not shown |
| Some reviews | Medium | Score with confidence range |
| Many reviews | Good | Reliable score |
| Extensive reviews | High | High-confidence score |

## ⏳ Time Decay

Recent reviews matter more than old ones. If your service improved recently, your score will reflect that improvement over time — you're not permanently defined by past issues.

## 🔄 The Resolution Advantage

Companies that actively respond to and resolve issues score significantly better than companies with the same average rating that ignore feedback. The resolution loop is your strongest tool:

1. Customer reports issue in 5R★ Reviews
2. You respond publicly
3. Issue gets resolved
4. Customer updates their review
5. Your 5R★ Trust Score improves

## 🤖 Fake Review Protection

Our 5R★ AI Risk Flags system identifies and reduces the weight of fake reviews — both fake positive and fake negative. This protects your score from:
- Competitor attacks (fake negative reviews)
- Astroturfing (fake positive reviews from affiliates)
- Review bombing campaigns

Legitimate reviews are never affected by fake detection.
